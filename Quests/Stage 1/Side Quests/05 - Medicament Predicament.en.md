# Medicament Predicament

## Summary

Flora, a little girl at the door of Runne's Apothecary in Melve, needs a Fruit Roborant to treat someone in the village.

## How to Unlock

Available on arrival at Melve during [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Flora
- **Location**: [[Locations/Melve]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]] *(started)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Flora at the door of Runne's Apothecary
2. Get a Fruit Roborant:
3.   • **Craft**: combine Dried Fruit + Greenwarish
4.   • **Buy**: 600 G at the Apothecary
5. Deliver the Fruit Roborant to Flora

## Objectives

- [x] Talk to Flora
- [x] Get the Fruit Roborant
- [x] Deliver it to Flora

## Rewards

| Item |
|---|
| 500 XP |
| 100 G |
| Ring of Exultation |

## Important Notes

- Crafting is much cheaper than buying — Greenwarish is abundant around Melve
- Ring of Exultation increases resistance to negative status effects
- Take the chance to buy **Fulminous Shield** from **Dudley** (local vendor)
