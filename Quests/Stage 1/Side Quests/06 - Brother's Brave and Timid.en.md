# Brother's Brave and Timid

## Summary

Ian, a shy boy from Melve, is worried about his brother Norbert who went into Backwoods Nest and is surrounded by wolves. You escort Ian to the cave and rescue Norbert.

## How to Unlock

Available on arrival at Melve during [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Ian
- **Location**: [[Locations/Melve]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]] *(started)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Ian in front of Lennart's house
2. Follow Ian toward Backwoods Nest (cave north of the village)
3. Protect Ian from wolf ambushes on the way
4. In the cave, find Norbert surrounded by a wolf pack
5. Defeat all the wolves quickly
6. Escort Ian and Norbert back to Melve

## Objectives

- [x] Talk to Ian
- [x] Escort Ian to the nest
- [x] Defeat the wolves
- [x] Rescue Norbert
- [x] Return with the brothers

## Important Notes

- **Don't stray from Ian** — he's weak and dies easily
- Use area-of-effect spells to wipe the pack quickly (High Ingle, Levitate)
- Norbert is in real danger during combat
