# The Provisioner's Plight

## Summary

Geoffrey, Borderwatch's quartermaster, is out of supplies. You deliver a note to Markus on the northern cliff and gather provisions.

## How to Unlock

Available on arrival at Borderwatch after [[Main Quests/02 - Tale's Beginning]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Geoffrey
- **Location**: [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/02 - Tale's Beginning]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Geoffrey at the Borderwatch storehouse
2. Receive the Provisioner's Note
3. Climb up to Markus, on the cliff north of the outpost
4. Deliver the note — Markus asks for provisions before helping
5. Collect 3x Harspuds (harvest nearby plants or buy at the shop)
6. Craft 2x Salubrious Draughts: grapes/apples + Greenwarish
7. Return to Markus and hand over the provisions (receive Waking Powder)
8. Report back to Geoffrey to finish

## Objectives

- [x] Talk to Geoffrey
- [x] Deliver the note to Markus
- [x] Collect Harspuds
- [x] Craft Salubrious Draughts
- [x] Return to Geoffrey

## Rewards

| Item |
|---|
| 900 G |
| Dried Fruit x1 |
| Waking Powder (from Markus) |

## Important Notes

- **Take the chance to buy 3 swords from Kassandra** (the outpost's merchant) — you'll need them in `Claw Them Into Shape` (Stage 2)
- Grapes and Apples are interchangeable in the Salubrious Draught recipe
- Gather Greenwarish (green bushes) around the outpost — crafting is much cheaper than buying
