# Ordeal's of a New Recruit

## Summary

Phill, a soldier at Borderwatch's south gate, asks for help rescuing his brother (a recruit) being attacked by harpies on the southern road.

## How to Unlock

Available on arrival at Borderwatch after [[Main Quests/02 - Tale's Beginning]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Phill
- **Location**: [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/02 - Tale's Beginning]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Phill at the south gate of Borderwatch
2. Follow the southern trail — the marker shows the location
3. Find the recruit surrounded by harpies
4. Defeat the swarm of harpies (3-5 units) as quickly as possible
5. Return to Borderwatch and report to Phill

## Objectives

- [x] Talk to Phill
- [x] Save Phill's brother
- [x] Defeat the harpies
- [x] Report to Phill

## Rewards

| Item |
|---|
| Small XP |
| Moderate Gold |

## Important Notes

- **Timed quest** — the recruit can die if you take too long
- Use area-of-effect attacks to clear the swarm quickly
- Can be done in parallel with [[04 - The Provisioner's Plight]]
