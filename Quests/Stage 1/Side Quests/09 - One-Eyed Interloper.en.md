# One-Eyed Interloper

## Summary

A camouflaged Cyclops attacks you and Gregor on the road to Vernworth. Kill it quickly before it kills any party NPC.

## How to Unlock

Triggers automatically during [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]], on the road between Melve and Vernworth (near a rock barricade).

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Automatic trigger
- **Location**: [[Locations/Melve]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]] *(started)*

## Walkthrough

1. Leave Melve following Gregor toward Vernworth
2. On the road, there's a rock barricade — the group halts
3. The camouflaged Cyclops appears (it was hidden in the scenery)
4. Engage the Cyclops immediately — it will attack nearby NPCs
5. Use burst attacks (Mage, Magick Archer) for fast damage
6. After defeating it, the journey to Vernworth resumes

## Objectives

- [ ] Defeat the Cyclops
- [ ] Keep Gregor alive
- [ ] Continue to Vernworth

## Important Notes

- **Speed is critical** — the Cyclops can kill Gregor and the soldiers
- Stick close to Brant/the party before the trigger
- Area-of-effect spells (High Bolide, Mystic Bolt) deal massive damage
- Stagger abuse is useful: stagger the Cyclops for bonus damage
- Possible outcomes: 3 (with/without casualties, leaving Gregor to fight alone)
