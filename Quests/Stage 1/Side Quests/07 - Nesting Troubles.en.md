# Nesting Troubles

## Summary

Lennart, a villager near the well in Melve, asks you to destroy Saurian eggs in the nest across the river with fire, and then to poison the nest.

## How to Unlock

Available on arrival at Melve during [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Lennart
- **Location**: [[Locations/Melve]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]] *(started)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Lennart near the central well of Melve
2. Part 1 — Destroy the eggs with fire:
3.   • Head to the riverbank (right exit of the village)
4.   • Grab the fire barrels near the bank
5.   • Throw the barrels at the eggs across the river
6. Report to Lennart — he activates Part 2
7. Part 2 — Poison the nest:
8.   • Grab the poison flask near the village entrance
9.   • Throw it at the nest across the river
10. Report to Lennart to finish

## Objectives

- [x] Talk to Lennart
- [x] Destroy the eggs with fire
- [x] Grab the poison
- [x] Poison the nest
