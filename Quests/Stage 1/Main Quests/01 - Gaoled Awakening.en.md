# Gaoled Awakening

## Summary

You wake up in a cell with no memory. A beastren (the Pathfinder) frees you and you flee together through the mine to the surface.

## How to Unlock

The first quest of the game. No prerequisites — it starts automatically.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: The Pathfinder
- **Location**: [[Locations/Excavation Site]]

## Prerequisites

- None (intro quest)

## Walkthrough

1. Exit the cell through the newly-opened gate and follow Fiska (the overseer) to the camp
2. Mark the quest as Priority in the menu so the marker shows on the minimap
3. Follow Fiska, then Rook, down to the cave at the bottom of the camp
4. Pick up a stone using Grab (R2) and carry it back to Rook
5. Return to the camp — a cutscene shows Medusa attacking
6. Climb onto Medusa (Grab) and strike her head (weak point)
7. When Medusa flees, follow Rook and leap off the cliff to escape
8. A long cutscene ends the quest — it automatically continues into Tale's Beginning
