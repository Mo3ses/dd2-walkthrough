# In Dragon's Wake

## Summary

Brant escorts you from Borderwatch to Melve. On arrival, the Dragon attacks the village. You survive the attack and continue on the road toward Vernworth, escorted by the knight Gregor.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[Main Quests/02 - Tale's Beginning]] and (recommended) the Borderwatch side quests.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant
- **Location**: [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/02 - Tale's Beginning]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. In Borderwatch, accept Brant's escort to Melve
2. Leave through the south gate and follow the path (ignore the eastern fork — dead end)
3. Pick up Gold Trove Beetles along the way (each one = +0.15 carry capacity)
4. Cross the bridge to Melve — the village is partially destroyed
5. Cutscene of the dragon-attack flashback — survive for ~1 minute
6. Wake up in Ulrika's house — talk to her
7. Speak with the borderwatch and gather soldiers to escort you to Vernworth
8. Explore Melve freely — chest with a Wakestone Shard in Ulrika's house
9. Visit the inn to talk to Lennart (Ulrika's father) — earns 500 XP + 2,500 G
10. Follow Gregor to the Checkpoint — don't lose him!

## Objectives

> [!info] Two-part structure
> This quest has **2 parts**. The first 3 objectives (up to "Talk to Lennart") are the normal flow from Borderwatch → Melve. The last 2 (Escort Gregor + Arrive in Vernworth) **can only be completed during [[Side Quests/09 - One-Eyed Interloper]]** — the side quest triggers automatically on the road and Gregor must survive.

- [ ] Head to Melve
- [ ] Explore Melve
- [ ] Talk to Lennart
- --- during [[Side Quests/09 - One-Eyed Interloper]] ---
- [ ] Escort Gregor to the capital
- [ ] Arrive in Vernworth

## Important Notes

- **Don't try to kill the Dragon** — its damage output is too high for your level
- **Gregor must survive** — if he dies, you can't pass through Northern Vermund Checkpoint
- If you reach Vernworth without Gregor, the quest still completes (with consequences)
- During the escape, use Curatives / Recovery Stones to survive
- On the road, **One-Eyed Interloper** triggers when a camouflaged cyclops attacks
