# Tale's Beginning

## Summary

The Arisen journeys with the Pathfinder to the Ultramarine Waterfall, where the Brine's voice manifests. Then they travel with Brant to the military post at Borderwatch.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[Main Quests/01 - Gaoled Awakening]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant
- **Location**: [[Locations/Ultramarine Waterfall]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/01 - Gaoled Awakening]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Follow the river upstream to the Ultramarine Waterfall
2. Contemplate the waterfall — a cutscene plays about the Seneschal's cycle
3. Customize your Main Pawn (appearance, vocation can be changed later)
4. Follow the trail to Borderwatch Outpost
5. Meet Brant (Captain of the Guard) at the Borderwatch entrance
6. Meet the soldiers at the post: Klaus, Phill, Geoffrey, Markus
7. Brant offers an escort to Melve — accept it after completing the side quests
