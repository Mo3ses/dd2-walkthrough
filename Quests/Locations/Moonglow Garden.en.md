# Moonglow Garden

> Quiet garden on the outskirts of [[Locations/Vernworth]], kept by master archer **Beren** as a training and retreat space. Destination of two quests: `Claw Them Into Shape` (needs swords bought in Stage 1) and `Beren's Final Lesson`.

## Progression Position

> [!info] **When to visit**
> Moonglow Garden is visited **after completing Vernworth Block 2** (after `House of the Blue Sunbright`) and **before `Spellbound`**.

## Characteristics

- **Owner**: **Beren** — master archer / mentor
- **Facilities**: training targets, combat areas, botanical garden
- **Atmosphere**: peaceful, isolated — ideal for archer training
- **Preparation for `Claw Them Into Shape`**:
    - ⚠️ **Buy 3 swords** from Kassandra at [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]] during Stage 1

## Stage 2 Quests Here

- [[34 - Claw Them Into Shape]] — side (needs 3 swords — Stage 1 prep)
- [[35 - Beren's Final Lesson]] — main

## Important Notes

> [!warning] **Cross-Stage Prep**
> `Claw Them Into Shape` requires 3 swords bought at [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]] during Stage 1 (from Kassandra). **If you didn't buy them**, the quest cannot be completed.

> [!tip] **Beren's Full Chain**
> Completing `Claw Them Into Shape` before `Beren's Final Lesson` unlocks extra dialogue.
