# Excavation Site (Abandoned Mines)

> An abandoned mine where the **Arisen wakes up imprisoned** at the start of the game. The setting of [[Gaoled Awakening]] and the game's tutorial/intro.

## Features

- **Geography**: a complex of underground tunnels, partially collapsed chambers, and a cell system
- **Atmosphere**: dark, humid, with little natural light
- **Inhabitants**: nobody — only you, and **the Pathfinder** (a beastren) who shows up to free you

## Important NPCs

- **The Pathfinder (Brant)** — leader of the Brines / Brigand's Pathfinders expedition; helps the Arisen escape the cells
- Note: the original Brant from Brine is who accompanies you on the escape

## Stage 1 Quests Here

- [[Gaoled Awakening]] — main quest, opening the game (escape from the mine)

## Important Notes

- You **cannot freely return** to Excavation Site after the start — it only becomes available again much later in the story
- The escape through the labyrinth is highly scripted (first linear quest)
- This is the only quest where **there is no significant combat** — pure tutorial/exploration
- Useful loot: a few chests with gems/materials in the nearby cells

## Lore

Excavation Site was a legendary mining operation the Pathfinders were excavating. The Arisen was found trapped/encapsulated in red crystal for centuries, since the events of the first game (Dragon's Dogma 1).
