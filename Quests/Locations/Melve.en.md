# Melve

> A rural village in **northern Vermund**, founded around a central well and home to several important characters in the early story (Brant, Wilksa, etc.). It is the **last location** of Stage 1 — visited after Borderwatch.

## Position in the Progression

> [!info] **When to visit**
> Melve is the **4th and last location** of Stage 1:
> 1. [[Locations/Excavation Site]]
> 2. [[Locations/Ultramarine Waterfall]]
> 3. [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]]
> 4. **[[Locations/Melve]]** ← you are here

## Features

- **Leader / Elder**: **Wilksa** (local ruler)
- **Notable villagers**:
    - **Wilksa** — elder, key piece of the early plot
    - **Aldara** — general shop
    - **Dreil** — blacksmith / weapon smith
    - **Flora** — "little girl" at the door of the Apothecary, offers [[05 - Medicament Predicament]]
    - **Lennart** — villager near the well, offers [[07 - Nesting Troubles]]
    - **Ian** and **Norbert** — brothers (involved in [[06 - Brother's Brave and Timid]])
    - **Dudley** — local vendor (sells books/components, including the grimoire `Fulminous Shield`)
- **Facilities**:
    - **Runne's Apothecary** — at the village entrance, sells and combines medicines
    - **Central well** — gathering point for villagers
    - **Blacksmith (Dreil)** — weapons/armor of the basic vocations
- **Atmosphere**: rural, simple, peaceful (before the Dragon event)

## Stage 1 Quests Here

- [[08 - In Dragon's Wake]] — main quest, **the Dragon attacks Melve** during your arrival
- [[05 - Medicament Predicament]] — side, Flora at the Apothecary
- [[06 - Brother's Brave and Timid]] — side, Ian at Lennart's house
- [[07 - Nesting Troubles]] — side, Lennart near the well
- [[09 - One-Eyed Interloper]] — side, NPC to be confirmed

## Important Notes

> [!info] **Arriving at Melve**
> Brant leads the trip from Borderwatch to Melve. On arrival, the Arisen is presented to Wilksa and the villagers, and during this same period the **Dragon event** occurs ([[08 - In Dragon's Wake]]).

> [!tip] **Melve's side quests are available during your stay**
> The 4 Melve side quests can be started **as soon as you arrive in the village** — they don't require a "return after the attack" or any additional prerequisite beyond being in Melve.

## Mental Map

- **Runne's Apothecary**: at the village entrance, on the left
- **Blacksmith (Dreil)**: opposite side from the main entrance
