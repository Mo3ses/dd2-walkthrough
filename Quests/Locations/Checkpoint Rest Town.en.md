# Checkpoint Rest Town

> Small resting settlement along the **main road between [[Locations/Vernworth]] and Battahl**. Three Stage 2 quests happen here: `Prey for the Pack`, `Hunt for the Jadeite Orb`, `The Sorcerer's Appraisal` — all related to Magick / Sorcerer vocation prep.

## Progression Position

> [!info] **When to visit**
> After `The Ailing Arborheart` (north exit from Vernworth) — last stop before Stage 3.

## Characteristics

- **Function**: rest stop, military / merchant outpost
- **NPCs**: Various mages / scholars (Roderick the Wicked — TBD)
- **Atmosphere**: transitional, commercial
- **Facilities**: Inn (main), multiple vendors

## Stage 2 Quests Here

- [[41 - Prey for the Pack]] — side
- [[42 - Hunt for the Jadeite Orb]] — side
- [[43 - The Sorcerer's Appraisal]] — side

## Important Notes

> [!info] **Magick / Sorcerer chain**
> Checkpoint Rest Town is the last pre-Stage 3 chance to farm Magick materials and train Sorcerer vocation. Don't skip if building a sorcerer.

> [!tip] **Rest before Stage 3**
> Inn here is cheaper than in Vernworth. Stock up on consumables and rest before heading to Battahl in Stage 3.
