# Borderwatch Outpost

> A border military post located in **northern Vermund**, on the edge of the wilds. It is the **first major civilized point** the Arisen reaches after escaping [[Excavation Site]] and passing through [[Ultramarine Waterfall]].

## Position in the Progression

> [!info] **When to visit**
> Borderwatch is the **3rd location** of Stage 1:
> 1. [[Locations/Excavation Site]]
> 2. [[Locations/Ultramarine Waterfall]]
> 3. **[[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]]** ← you are here
> 4. [[Locations/Melve]]

## Features

- **Main NPCs**:
    - **Klaus** — veteran leader/pathfinder at the outpost (remembers the Arisen from childhood and the reason he was in the mines)
    - **Phill** — soldier at the south gate, offers [[03 - Ordeal's of a New Recruit]]
    - **Geoffrey** — quartermaster, offers [[04 - The Provisioner's Plight]]
    - **Markus** — gatherer/hunter on a cliff north of the outpost
    - **Kassandra** — local merchant (sells weapons, supplies; **buy 3 swords here** for the Stage 2 quest `Claw Them Into Shape`)
- **Facilities**: inn, blacksmith, storehouse, fortified gates
- **Atmosphere**: military, functional — soldiers on patrol, a few villagers in the surroundings

## Stage 1 Quests Here

- [[03 - Ordeal's of a New Recruit]] — Phill, at the south gate
- [[04 - The Provisioner's Plight]] — Geoffrey, at the storehouse

## Important Notes

> [!warning] **Borderwatch side quests do not return afterwards**
> Do [[03 - Ordeal's of a New Recruit]] and [[04 - The Provisioner's Plight]] **before** leaving for [[Melve]] with Brant. There is no return to this outpost in Stage 1.

> [!tip] **[[03 - Ordeal's of a New Recruit]] is timed**
> Don't linger around the outpost. When you activate the quest, leave immediately via the south gate and follow the quest marker.

> [!info] **Lore**
> Klaus mentions your childhood and the reason you were in [[Excavation Site]] — dialogue relevant to the Arisen's backstory.

## Next Destination

From Borderwatch, Brant offers an **escort to [[Melve]]**. Accept when you finish the two side quests:
- Going to Melve triggers [[08 - In Dragon's Wake]] (Dragon event)
