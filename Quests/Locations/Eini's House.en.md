# Eini's House

> Modest home of **Eini** and her daughter **Trysha**, on the outskirts of [[Locations/Vernworth]]. Trysha offers the quest `Spellbound` to the Arisen — one of the few character-driven side quests early in Stage 2.

## Progression Position

> [!info] **When to visit**
> After `Beren's Final Lesson` (leaving Moonglow Garden) — makes the Moonglow→Eini's House transition natural.

## Characteristics

- **Residents**:
    - **Eini** — Trysha's mother, works away
    - **Trysha** — daughter, offers `Spellbound`
- **Atmosphere**: domestic, calm

## Stage 2 Quests Here

- [[36 - Spellbound]] — side, Trysha as quest giver

## Important Notes

> [!tip] **Trysha's chain**
> `Spellbound` opens extra interactions with Trysha throughout Vernworth. Completing early gives cumulative rewards.
