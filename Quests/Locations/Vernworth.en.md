# Vernworth

> Capital of the **kingdom of Vermund**, residence of the Sovran and primary urban hub. After escaping Melve with Gregor, the Arisen arrives in Vernworth — most of Stage 2 and Stage 3 quests are anchored here.

## Progression Position

> [!info] **When to visit**
> Vernworth is visited in **multiple waves** during Stage 2 (and returns in Stage 3):
> 1. [[Locations/Excavation Site]]
> 2. [[Locations/Ultramarine Waterfall]]
> 3. [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]]
> 4. [[Locations/Melve]]
> 5. **[[Locations/Vernworth]]** ← **Stage 2 starts here**

## Characteristics

- **Ruler**: **Sovran Disa** (regent queen, mother of the false Arisen)
- **Castle**: [[Locations/Vernworth Castle]] — royal residence, throne room
- **Districts**:
    - **Merchant Quarter** — smithy (Sven), item shop
    - **Slums** — poor area, focus of several side quests
    - **Tavern District** — main inn, social encounters
    - **Nobles' Quarter** — houses of Brant, Sigurd, other officers
- **Key NPCs**: Brant (Captain of the Guard), Roderick (Sovran / false Arisen), Sven (smith), Kendrick, Allard, Luz, Martin, Sigurd, Nadina, Della, Gautstafr, Doireann, Ser Berthold, Glyndwr
- **Facilities**: Castle, Smithy (Sven), Shop, Inn, Guard camps

## Important Notes

> [!info] **Stage 1 → Stage 2 transition**
> Arrival at Vernworth is the turning point. `Seat of the Sovran` auto-triggers when crossing the gates. The Cyclops from `[[09 - One-Eyed Interloper]]` attacks the road immediately before.

> [!tip] **Equipment shopping in Vernworth**
> Sven (smith) sells mid-game weapons and upgrades. Hand in `Heel of History` material before buying to maximize stats.

> [!warning] **Vernworth side quests: many become missable after Stage 2**
> Check the Stage 2 MOC for which side quests lock out after certain events.
