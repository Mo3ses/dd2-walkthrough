# Ultramarine Waterfall

> A hidden waterfall in the mountains, mystical sanctuary where the **Arisen receives a Main Pawn** and has the first vision of the Brine. Setting of [[Tale's Beginning]].

## Features

- **Geography**: a main waterfall with a river feeding into a basin, surrounded by ancient stone ruins
- **Atmosphere**: mystical, with bluish light effects (suggestive of the "Ultramarine" name)
- **Importance**: high — a sacred site where the Arisen's Seneschal is contemplated and the "loop" cycle can be seen

## NPCs / Entities

- **Brine's Voice / Spectral Pawn** — a spectral figure that appears and shows you your first Pawn
- **Main Pawn (yours)** — you choose its appearance during the quest
- **Brant** — the captain of Vernworth's guard who finds you after this location and invites you to the city

## Stage 1 Quests Here

- [[Tale's Beginning]] — main quest, the Arisen's first journey + Main Pawn creation

## Important Notes

- Visited **only during Tale's Beginning** — you don't normally return here
- Your Main Pawn choice here defines your companion for the rest of the game
- After this quest the game "opens" — you gain total freedom of exploration
- The waterfall is **south of the camp** — follow the river downstream to the falls

## Lore

The Ultramarine Waterfall was a site of worship to the **Seneschal** in the ancient kingdom of Vermund. The place keeps vestiges of the older religion of the region and is connected to the Sphere/Cycle that the Arisen inhabits.
