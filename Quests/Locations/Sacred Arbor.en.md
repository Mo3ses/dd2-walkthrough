# Sacred Arbor

> Sacred grove to the **north of [[Locations/Vernworth]]**, home of an ancient treant called **Arom**. The Arisen visits for the quest `The Ailing Arborheart` — diagnosing and curing the tree's ailment.

## Progression Position

> [!info] **When to visit**
> After `A Trial of Archery` (Vernworth Block 3) — last stop before Checkpoint Rest Town.

## Characteristics

- **Resident**: **Arom** — Ancient Treant, focus of `The Ailing Arborheart`
- **Atmosphere**: sacred, lush, with rare flora
- **Facilities**: rest spots, wooded paths

## Stage 2 Quests Here

- [[40 - The Ailing Arborheart]] — main

## Important Notes

> [!warning] **Arom's condition**
> Time is relevant in this quest (not critical-path but avoids missable rewards). See notes on [[40 - The Ailing Arborheart|quest]].

> [!info] **Stage 4 connection**
> Sacred Arbor returns in Stage 4 with `Wandering Roots`. Familiarity with Arom gives extra dialogue here.
