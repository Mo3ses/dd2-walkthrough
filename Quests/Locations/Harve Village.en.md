# Harve Village

> Small coastal village in **southwest Vermund**, home to fishermen and the closest port to the capital. The Arisen visits Harve twice during Stage 2: once for `Scaly Invaders` and once for `Trouble on the Cape`.

## Progression Position

> [!info] **When to visit (2 trips in Stage 2)**
> 1. **1st visit**: After Vernworth Block 1 (after `Monster Culling`)
> 2. **2nd visit**: After `Beren's Final Lesson` (mid-game Stage 2)

## Characteristics

- **Economy**: fishing, small trade port
- **Key NPCs**:
    - **Oskar** — villager / quest giver `Scaly Invaders`
    - **Symon** — fisherman / quest giver `Trouble on the Cape`
- **Facilities**: Inn, small port, fisher houses
- **Atmosphere**: coastal, calm, with coastal creatures nearby

## Stage 2 Quests Here

- [[18 - Scaly Invaders]] — side (1st visit)
- [[37 - Trouble on the Cape]] — side (2nd visit)

## Important Notes

> [!info] **NPCs and open world**
> Harve doesn't have many quests beyond the two above; it's a quick stopover from [[Locations/Vernworth]]. Useful as a fast-travel point.

> [!warning] **Scaly Invaders timing**
> Correct timing for `Scaly Invaders` prevents losing side NPCs in the scene. See notes on the [[18 - Scaly Invaders|quest]].
