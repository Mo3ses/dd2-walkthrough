# Ancient Battleground

Location **north of [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]]**, marked by ancient ruins and the presence of the **Sphinx** (see `A Game of Wits`).

## Points of interest

- **Battleground entrance** — where elder Oscar is held captive (see `Tolled To Rest`).
- **Central cenotaph** — chest with `Nation's Death Knell` grimoire (1 of 2 originals in the game).
- **Sphinx cave** — pedestal for the 5 riddles.
- **Riftstone of Fellowship** — for renaming pawns (need a pawn called `SphinxParent` for Riddle of Wisdom).
- **Western campsite** — where Beren appears after `Claw Them Into Shape` in Stage 2.

## Important Notes

- **Cross-Stage Carry**: part of the `Tolled To Rest` + `A Game of Wits` chain; can be initiated via the `Till Death Do Us Part` continuation.
- First Seeker's Tokens stay hidden until `Tolled To Rest` is completed.
