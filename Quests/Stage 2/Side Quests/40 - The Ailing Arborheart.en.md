# The Ailing Arborheart

## Summary

The ancient treant **Arom** at the Sacred Arbor is sick. Diagnose the cause and obtain curatives. Quest located in the far north of Vernworth.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[39 - A Trial of Archery]] and travel to [[Locations/Sacred Arbor]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Arom
- **Location**: [[Locations/Sacred Arbor]]

## Prerequisites

- [[39 - A Trial of Archery]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to **Sacred Arbor** and talk to **Doireann** at the **Arborheart** — she explains the great tree is dying and only "**gwyfencha**" can cure it.
2. **Find out what gwyfencha is** — three paths:
   - **Easiest:** have a Pawn with **Woodland Wordsmith** specialization (tome from Kough's Inn via Bunches of Flowers, OR hire one at the Arborheart Riftstone)
   - **Alternative:** talk to **Glyndwr** at his training grounds
   - **Long route:** complete "Put a Spring in Thy Step," then revisit **Gautstafr**
3. **Translation revealed:** gwyfencha = **Scalecinder**.
4. Travel to **Bakbattahl** and talk to **Sara** at **Brokkr's Smithy** (Brokkr is grumpy / anti-elf).
   - **Requirement:** Sara only helps if **Steeled Resolve, Blazing Forge** is completed.
5. **Sara** agrees to procure **Scalecinder**, but notes Brokkr's elven prejudice — suggests the **elven dish *nutriabh*** to placate him. This unlocks the follow-up **"Out of the Forest, Into the Forge."**

## Objectives

- [ ] Go to the Sacred Arbor
- [ ] Talk to Arom
- [ ] Investigate the symptoms
- [ ] Collect curatives / antidotes
- [ ] Cure Arom

## Rewards

- 1,600 XP (base quest)
- Follow-up "Out of the Forest, Into the Forge": 13,000 G, **Warbler Capelet**, 6× Scalecinder
