# Disa's Plot

## Summary

Political quest tied to Sovran Disa. Investigate behind-the-scenes conspiracies of power in Vernworth — choices during the investigation affect [[23 - The Stolen Throne]].

## How to Unlock

Start [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and talk to NPCs near Brant / castle.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] *(started)*

## Walkthrough

1. Triggered during Seat of the Sovran: choose "Tell me of the queen regent" when talking to Brant at The Stardrop Inn.
2. Go to the back gate of the castle between midnight and dawn.
3. One of Brant's guards will identify you and ask you to follow — stay close until you reach the Guardhouse.
4. Investigate the Guardhouse (restricted area — discretion).
5. Gather evidence of Queen Regent Disa's actions.
6. Report to Brant in Vernworth.

## Objectives

- [ ] Talk to key NPCs about Disa
- [ ] Gather evidence
- [ ] Confront or report the conspiracy
