# A Case of Sculptor's Block

## Summary

Klark, a sculptor in the noble quarter of Vernworth, suffers from creative block. Help him overcome it by commissioning a masterwork.

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to **Klark** in the noble quarter of Vernworth (in his workshop).
2. If the quest isn't available, try after [[23 - The Stolen Throne]].

## Rewards

- 4,500 XP
- 18,500 G (sculpted perfectly) or 7,000 G (sculpted poorly)
