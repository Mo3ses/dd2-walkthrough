# The Ornate Box

## Summary

An ornate box of mysterious origin is delivered to the Arisen. Investigate the contents, where it came from, and what the original owner wants with it.

## How to Unlock

Start [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and explore [[Locations/Vernworth]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC (verify)
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. The quest starts when you overhear a discussion in the Merchant Quarter between a merchant and a customer.
2. Approach them — The Ornate Box activates automatically.
3. Talk to the young man who needs money to buy the Ornate Box from the merchant (1,000 G per IGN).
4. Pay the requested amount and obtain the Ornate Box.
5. Return to Sven a few days later to receive the reward (Fextralife).

## Objectives

- [ ] Receive the ornate box
- [ ] Investigate the contents
- [ ] Discover the sender
- [ ] Deliver to the correct recipient
