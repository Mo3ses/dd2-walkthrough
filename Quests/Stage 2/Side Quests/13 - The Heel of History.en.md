# The Heel of History

## Summary

Lore quest involving historical artifacts of the royal lineage and the Arisen. Gathering materials and deciphering clues connects to the game's true ending.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[12 - Oxcart Courier]] and speak with a scholar NPC (verify Glyndwr).

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Glyndwr (?) — verify
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[12 - Oxcart Courier]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Kendrick (Vernworth) about Malcolm — a missing child.
2. Ask the children in the Slums, near the Gracious Hand: Harvey (north of the church), and two others.
3. Investigate the clues to determine Malcolm's location.
4. Search nearby vaults / dungeons.
5. Find Malcolm and discover his condition.
6. Report to Kendrick.
