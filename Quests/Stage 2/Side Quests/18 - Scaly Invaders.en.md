# Scaly Invaders

## Summary

Aquatic creatures (lizard / saurians) are attacking Harve Village. Oskar asks the Arisen to investigate and cull the invaders before more villagers are harmed.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and travel to [[Locations/Harve Village]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Oskar
- **Location**: [[Locations/Harve Village]]

## Prerequisites

- [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to Harve Village after A Place to Call Home.
2. Speak with Fyoran (village NPC) — saurians have occupied Harve since the dragon attack.
3. Cull the Saurians in the coastal region of the village.
4. Report to Fyoran after clearing the infestation.
5. Wait a few days — saurians return (Fextralife).
6. Cull them again and report back.
