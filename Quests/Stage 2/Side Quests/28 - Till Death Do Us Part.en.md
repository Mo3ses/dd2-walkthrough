# Till Death Do Us Part

## Summary

Romantic / character quest centered on Sigurd (retiree) and his wife Nadina. Help Sigurd deal with personal matters before an important event.

## How to Unlock

Start [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and talk to Sigurd in Vernworth.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Sigurd
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Noble Quarter in Vernworth. Near the fountain on the north side, find Oswald.
2. Oswald shares a rumor about Ser Ludolph and Gregor (the city's watchhead).
3. Ludolph apparently sent Gregor on a mission.
4. Talk to Margit (Fextralife) — quest giver.
5. Follow the trail to the Illdoers' Resting Place (exact location IGN).
6. Help Gregor survive — quest conclusion.
7. Report to Margit.
