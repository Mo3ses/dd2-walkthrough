# Beren's Final Lesson

## Summary

Beren's final lesson — climax of archer training at Moonglow Garden. Boss fight or final trial against a memorable enemy.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[34 - Claw Them Into Shape]] and talk to Beren.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Beren
- **Location**: [[Locations/Moonglow Garden]]

## Prerequisites

- [[34 - Claw Them Into Shape]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Wait **one day** after [[34 - Claw Them Into Shape]], then visit Beren at Moonglow Garden.
2. **Intercept the Cyclops** — during the conversation, **Randall** runs down the hill being chased. Defeat the Cyclops.
3. **Talk to Beren** after the fight. (If needed, sleep at the nearby campsite and return **the next morning**, before he leaves — miss the timing and lose rewards.)
4. Receive the **Lifetaker greatsword** — Beren mentions he is heading to Battahl to train soldiers.
5. **Follow him** to **Beren's Childhood Home** (south of Checkpoint Rest Town in Battahl).
6. **Beren gives you the Champion's Fable**, closing the quest.

> [!tip] Fallback
> If Beren **does not hand over** the tome: (a) exhaust his dialogue, then past Checkpoint Rest Town's gate → south past Zeplin's Inn → find him in a house on the left; OR (b) raise affinity high enough (escort quest, gifting flowers) until he blushes, then check Borderwatch Outpost or the childhood home.
