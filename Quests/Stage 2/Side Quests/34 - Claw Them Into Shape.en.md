# Claw Them Into Shape

## Summary

Beren (master archer) at Moonglow Garden asks the Arisen to shape 3 swords into claws for a training exercise. The 3 swords must be bought in **Stage 1** from Kassandra at Borderwatch Outpost.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and travel to [[Locations/Moonglow Garden]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Beren
- **Location**: [[Locations/Moonglow Garden]]

## Prerequisites

- [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] *(completed)*
- **Cross-Stage:** 3 swords bought from [[Kassandra]] at [[Locations/Borderwatch Outpost]] (Stage 1)

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to **Beren** at his tent south of **Moonglow Garden** (southwest of Borderwatch Outpost).
2. Buy **3 Swords** from Borderwatch Outpost vendors for **850 G each**. (Stage 1 — if you skipped it, **cannot recover**.)
3. Return to Beren and hand over the 3 swords.
4. Travel to Vernworth; **southeast of Clovis's Barberie** — find **Humphrey**. Choose the dialogue "**Tell him about Beren**".
5. Return to Borderwatch Outpost training grounds.
6. **Fight Beren in the ring** — who wins doesn't matter, but stay inside the bounds.
7. **Cutscene** triggers, and a soldier arrives reporting a **goblin attack**.
8. **Follow Beren** (runs from the outpost toward a western campsite).
9. **Stay on his trail** until you arrive at the campsite.
10. Find the **wounded soldier** to close the quest.

> [!warning] Known bug
> This quest is reported as getting stuck for several players; Beren may only appear at night at his tent. If the quest doesn't progress, return to Beren's tent at night.

## Objectives
