# Readvent of Calamity

## Summary

Side quest of returning to Melve. After the Dragon's attack in [[08 - In Dragon's Wake]], a new calamity threatens the village. Return to investigate and protect the villagers.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and travel back to [[Locations/Melve]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Melve NPC (Wilksa or another elder)
- **Location**: [[Locations/Melve]]

## Prerequisites

- [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Return to **Melve** a few in-game days after [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and the progress of [[18 - Scaly Invaders]] — a cutscene triggers with the **Dragon** attacking the village. Help Ulrika and the villagers repel it.
2. **Break the red bubbles** on the dragon's body for stagger / knockdown (heavy damage).
   - [!] **Warning:** **Do NOT fell the dragon here** — it may complicate obtaining the Mystic Spearhand maister skill from Sigurd. Felling it via the ballista at the watchtower grants Sigurd's reward (Paladins Enigmata if spared; Dead Ringer weapon if killed).
3. After the dragon flees (3rd health bar), **talk to Ulrika** who thanks you. **Speak briefly with Sigurd** (the man in red) to unlock **Mystic Spearhand Vocation**.
4. Return to Melve over time — find Ulrika in her house talking with **Martin**, who accuses her of **treason**. She asks you to stay the night.
5. The next morning, **Ulrika has fled to Harve Village** (southwest of Melve). **Lennart** asks you to locate her. Speaking to Ulrika in Harve completes her location.
6. **Report to Lennart** in Melve and inform him that Ulrika is safe at Harve Village.
