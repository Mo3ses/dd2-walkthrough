# Trouble on the Cape

## Summary

Problem on the coast of Harve Village — fisherman Symon reports an incident. Investigate and resolve it for the fishermen.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[36 - Spellbound]] and travel to [[Locations/Harve Village]] (2nd visit).

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Symon
- **Location**: [[Locations/Harve Village]]

## Prerequisites

- [[36 - Spellbound]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Enter **Harve Village** (2nd visit) — a cutscene shows **Fyoran** and **Jonas** arguing over a missing villager. **Ulrika** intervenes and runs toward **Stormwind Cave** (south) — auto-start.
2. **Follow Ulrika** into the cave. Eliminate the **Saurians**.
3. Turn **left** and head **east** to find the captured villager, **Zoran** — rescue him.
4. **Escort Zoran** back to Harve Village (don't use Oxcart — known drop bug; carry him if he wanders).
5. Return cutscene — Ulrika offers to leave, but Fyoran and Jonas ask her to become **Village Chief**. Quest complete.

> [!warning] Unlock window
> Ulrika needs to be **in Harve** for the quest to spawn — if you arrive too early, sleep or do other quests until she relocates from Melve (she came from [[32 - Readvent of Calamity]]).

> [!warning] Deadline
> **Finish** [[32 - Readvent of Calamity]] **before** [[44 - Feast of Deception]], or Ulrika **won't** become chief. Also report to Lennart in Melve to continue that quest.
