# Prey for the Pack

## Summary

Hunt the wolf pack near Checkpoint Rest Town. Quick combat side quest, useful for training the Ranger / Archer Vocation.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[40 - The Ailing Arborheart]] and follow the north road to Checkpoint Rest Town.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Checkpoint Rest Town]]

## Prerequisites

- [[40 - The Ailing Arborheart]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to **Checkpoint Rest Town**.
2. Talk to **Lutz**, then gather info from the townsfolk outside the **Sword and Staff**: **Timothy** (blue shirt / round hat), **Alsa** (green dress), **Jacint** (white dress / head cloth).
3. Learn the clues: **cenotaph east of town**, **Rodge** tends a flowerbed on the outskirts, **glowing flowers bloom at night**.
4. Follow **Guerco Mountain Road** past a bridge / oxcart, through the narrow river and tunnel (watch for **harpies**), to a **campfire above the cave entrance with glowing flowers**.
5. **DO NOT rest** at the campfire — passage of time can kill Rodge. Descend past the **Riftstone**; the **wooden bridge** marks the entrance to **Putrid Cave**.
6. **Rescue Rodge** from the wolves inside the cave.
7. **Escort him back on foot** (don't use Oxcarts — known bug). **Carry him** if he wanders; avoid dropping him.
8. **Deliver Rodge** to **Morris** in Checkpoint Rest Town.

## Objectives

- [ ] Talk to the quest giver in Checkpoint
- [ ] Locate the pack
- [ ] Cull all the wolves
- [ ] Return and report

## Rewards

- 4,000 XP
- 2× **Miracle Roborant**
- 11,000 G
- 5% shop discount at Morris's shop

(Failed: Rodge dies = 4,000 XP + 8,000 G (no items). Found but lost escort = 4,000 XP + 8,000 G only.)
