# The Gift of Giving

## Summary

Help NPCs in Vernworth with deliveries of gifts / small acts of generosity. Low-risk side quest, useful for reputation farming.

## How to Unlock

Start [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and talk to NPCs in the Merchant Quarter.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Daphne (NPC in Vernworth — confirm location).
2. Receive the list of requested items to cheer up the sisters at the Gracious Hand.
3. Requested items (Fextralife): flowers, Silver Ore (2× Gold Ore also requested later), and medicine to treat blight.
4. Collect each item and deliver to Daphne over the course of several days.
5. Return after the agreed deadline for the final reward.
