# House of the Blue Sunbright

## Summary

Political / social quest involving the noble Sunbright family in Vernworth. Enter the manor, investigate intrigues, and confront the truth.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and follow clues to the Sunbright manor.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]] (Sunbright Manor)

## Prerequisites

- [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Visit the **Townshend's Estate** in the Noble Quarter of Vernworth — **Diana** (maid) explains that Sebastian can't leave without his mother noticing.
2. Go to the **Slums** and find **Daphne** near the tent outside **The Gracious Hand**. Convince her to come back with you to the manor.
3. Escort Daphne to the manor — Sebastian asks the maid to dress her as a decoy so he can go out freely.
4. Help Sebastian prepare: give **TWO** items — a **cure for poison AND drowsiness** (Detoxifying Decoction + Waking Powder, or All Heal Elixir) + an **item that smells of cyclops** (Rugged Bone or Cyclops Eye).
5. Wait one cycle (a few in-game minutes) and return to the manor. Sebastian returns safely with a **Blue Sunbright**. Natasha (the mother) discovers the swap and dismisses Daphne.
6. Return to **The Gracious Hand** later — Diana, now fired, reveals that Daphne is Sebastian's **twin sister**.

> [!tip] Soft prerequisite
> This quest unlocks after [[14 - The Gift of Giving]] delivers Diana as part of the chain — check the cross-reference in [[14 - The Gift of Giving]] if the quest isn't available.
