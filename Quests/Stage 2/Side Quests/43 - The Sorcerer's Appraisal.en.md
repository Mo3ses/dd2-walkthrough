# The Sorcerer's Appraisal

## Summary

A mage at Checkpoint Rest Town evaluates the Arisen's magical abilities. Reward includes a spell or magical item. Recommended for Sorcerer / Magick Archer builds.

## How to Unlock

Arrive at [[Locations/Checkpoint Rest Town]] and talk to the mage.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Mage NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Checkpoint Rest Town]]

## Prerequisites

None directly; requires access to the checkpoint (which comes after [[40 - The Ailing Arborheart]]).

## Walkthrough

1. **Get Noble Clothing:** equip **Courtly Tunic + Courtly Breeches** (reward from [[11 - The Ornate Box]], Philbert's Sundries, or houses in the nobles' district). Headgear not required.
2. **Talk to Myrddin** in Checkpoint Rest Town wearing the noble clothing (or as a **Mage/Sorcerer**) to be invited in.
3. **Collect 3 Grimoires** — the same 5 as in [[36 - Spellbound]]; 3 are required:
   - *Let There Be Light* — Myrddin's home, upstairs desk (enter via outside balcony to avoid combat)
   - *Fulminous Shield* — buy from **Dudley** in Melve
   - *Nation's Death Knell* — chest near the cenotaph in Ancient Battleground (only **2 originals**; finish *Tolled to Rest* first)
   - *Howling Blizzard* — loot or trade with **Wendy** (give a forged *Fulminous Shield*)
   - *Towering Earth* — **Waldhar** at The Gracious Hand's Vaults
4. **Optional: duplicate via Ibrahim** — forgeries let you complete **Spellbound** + this one simultaneously (shared origin).
5. **Best path:**
   - Finish **[[36 - Spellbound]] FIRST** (give 3 originals to Trysha).
   - Equip the **Turquoise Ring** from Trysha.
   - Give Myrddin 3 originals or forgeries.
   - Rest ~2 days, return; confirm you learned **Meteoron** (came from Trysha → Myrddin); talk to **Eini** to restore his trust.
6. **Receive rewards** from Myrddin.

> [!warning] Cancellation
> Completing *Halls of the First Dawn* cancels this quest — finish first if it's open.

- Counts toward the **"Master of the Maistes"** trophy
