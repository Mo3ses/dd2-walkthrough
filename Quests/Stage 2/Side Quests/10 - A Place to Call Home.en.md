# A Place to Call Home

## Summary

After arriving in Vernworth with Gregor, the Arisen needs to find accommodation and settle in the capital. Brant guides the initial transition from a traveler's life to urban life.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]] and arrive at the gates of [[Locations/Vernworth]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Approach Mildred (woman with a purple hat) at the entrance of The Stardrop Inn in Vernworth — she will ask you to look after her house.
2. Go to her house (marked on the map) and protect the area for one in-game week.
3. Use the house freely as rest and storage during the week.
4. After the week, return and talk to Mildred — she decides to sell the house to fund her travels.
5. Buy the house for 20,000 G to finish the quest.

## Objectives

- [ ] Meet Brant at the gates
- [ ] Choose an accommodation (inn or room)
- [ ] Learn the main districts of the capital
- [ ] Establish temporary residence

## Rewards

- 500 XP
- Permanent house in Vernworth (cost: 20,000 G)
- Trophy "A House? In This Economy?"

## Important Notes

- Both sources classify as side quest.
- IGN classifies as side quest; Fextralife too.
- Despite the high price, the purchase reduces inn costs long-term.
