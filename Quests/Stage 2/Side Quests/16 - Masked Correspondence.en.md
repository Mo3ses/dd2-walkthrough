# Masked Correspondence

## Summary

Anonymous letters circulate through Vernworth — investigate who is sending them, what they contain, and why.

## How to Unlock

Start [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and explore the Merchant Quarter / Tavern District.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Pick up the quest from Sven (his chambers) during or near the Masquerade night in Vernworth.
2. Sven asks you to deliver a letter to Lord Patrick.
3. Find Lord Patrick at the Masquerade night in Vernworth Hall (exact location to confirm).
4. Deliver the letter.
5. Report back to Regentkin Sven.

- [ ] Find the first letter
