# Hunt for the Jadeite Orb

## Summary

Hunt / find a magical jadeite orb lost in the surroundings of Checkpoint Rest Town. Exploration and combat side quest.

## How to Unlock

Arrive at [[Locations/Checkpoint Rest Town]] and talk to NPCs.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Checkpoint Rest Town]]

## Prerequisites

None directly; requires access to the checkpoint (which comes after [[40 - The Ailing Arborheart]]).

## Walkthrough

1. Accept the quest from **Everard** (outside Celeste's Smithy) or **Offulve** (in front of the Sword and Staff Inn) at **Checkpoint Rest Town**. Talk to **both** during the quest.
2. Locate **Ibrahim's Scrap Store** — behind Everard's position, hidden behind a thick bush.
3. **Buy the Jadeite Orb** from Ibrahim for **7,500 G**.
4. **Optional duplication via Sphinx** (for best outcome):
   - Travel west from Checkpoint through Ancient Battleground to Worldsend Cavern.
   - Proceed to Shrine Corridor, climb to Mountain Shrine.
   - At the **Sphinx**, offer the Jadeite Orb during the **Riddle of Conviction** — receive 2.
5. **Deliver the Jadeite Orb(s)** — outcome options:
   - **A:** Genuine orbs → both Everard and Offulve (best outcome)
   - **B:** Genuine → Everard only; then talk to Offulve (give / refuse / deceive)
   - **C:** Genuine → Offulve first
   - **D:** Forgery from Ibrahim → Everard, then **bribe Ibrahim 12,000 G** to lie about authenticity
6. **Receive rewards** from the NPCs you dealt with honestly.

## Objectives

- [ ] Talk to the quest giver
- [ ] Get clues about the orb
- [ ] Find the jadeite orb
- [ ] Return to the quest giver

## Rewards

Varies by outcome — based on honest outcomes:

- **Genuine → both** (best): 2,000 XP, 15,000 G, **Ring of Skullduggery**, **Elite Camping Kit**
- **Genuine → Offulve only**: 1,000 XP, 3,000 G, **Elite Camping Kit**
- **Genuine → Everard only**: 1,000 XP, 12,000 G, **Ring of Skullduggery**
- **Forgery → Offulve**: 1,000 XP, 3,000 G, **Elite Camping Kit** (Offulve discovers later)
