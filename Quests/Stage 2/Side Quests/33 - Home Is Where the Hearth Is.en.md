# Home Is Where the Hearth Is

## Summary

Reconstruction quest: help Melve villagers restore their homes after the Dragon's attack. Gather materials, rebuild structures, and bring hope back to the village.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and travel back to [[Locations/Melve]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Melve NPC (verify — Ulrika?)
- **Location**: [[Locations/Melve]]

## Prerequisites

- [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to **Lennart** inside Ulrika's House in Melve — he asks you to procure **4 swords**.
2. Obtain 4 swords — either **buy ahead of time** (from any blacksmith) OR **recover confiscated weapons** from the building west of the town gate.
3. Return the 4 swords to Lennart.
4. **Exit Ulrika's House** — this triggers a **fight with soldiers** (pawns unavailable; villagers assist).
5. After the fight, Melve villagers **relocate to Harve Village under Ulrika**.
6. Wait a few days (travel to other quests or rest).
7. Go to Harve Village and talk to Ulrika **at the dock at night** — use the inn or a bench to pass time if needed.

> [!tip] Setup
> Place a **Portcrystal in Melve** before starting — makes quick return easier if the quest bugs.
