# A Game of Wits

## Summary

The Sphinx proposes a sequence of riddles to the Arisen. Complete them all to unlock the sorcerer maister skill.

## Walkthrough

### Phase 1 — Riddle of Eyes

1. Pick up the **Sealing Phial** from the chest above the cave entrance (visible from the Sphinx entrance).

### Phase 2 — Riddle of Madness

2. Place your **Main Pawn** on the pedestal (the Pawn is "sacrificed" temporarily).

### Phase 3 — Riddle of Wisdom

3. Place the pawn named **SphinxParent** on the pedestal (you need a pawn with this name in the party — rename one at Riftstone of Fellowship).

### Phase 4 — Riddle of Rumination

4. After the other riddles, a **7-day in-game timer** starts. Return to the Ancient Battleground where you collected the first Seeker's Token and pick up the **Finder's Token** (sleep first if it doesn't appear).
5. Talk to the Sphinx to begin Riddle of Rumination — philosophical reflections.

### Phase 5 — Riddle of Conviction

6. Give the **Jadeite Orb** (from `Hunt for the Jadeite Orb`) to the Sphinx.
7. Afterwards you receive the **Sorcerer Maister Skill** + Enchanted attire.
