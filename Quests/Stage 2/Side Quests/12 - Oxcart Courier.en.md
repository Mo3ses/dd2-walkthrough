# Oxcart Courier

## Summary

Brant asks the Arisen to make a delivery via oxcart inside or outside Vernworth. Start of a sub-chain tied to Disa / Brant. Be careful: choices during transit have narrative consequences.

## How to Unlock

Start [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and speak with Brant.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] *(started)*

## Walkthrough

1. Find Donovan at the eastern gate of Vernworth (after Brant's first requests).
2. Donovan asks you to deliver a letter to Ser Lennart in Melve.
3. Take the oxcart in the morning (allowed travel window); Donovan pays the fare.
4. If this is your first time speaking to Lennart, this triggers the side quest Nesting Troubles.
5. Arrive in Melve, find Lennart near the well, and make the delivery.
6. Return to Donovan and report the delivery.

## Objectives

- [ ] Speak with Brant
- [ ] Find the designated oxcart
- --- after arriving at Melve ---
- [ ] Escort the transport to its destination
- [ ] Report to Brant

## Rewards

- 400 XP
- Miner's Hosen
- 500 G

## Important Notes

- Fextralife: picking up the quest during the day guarantees immediate oxcart boarding.
- Can trigger Nesting Troubles if it's your 1st interaction with Lennart.
