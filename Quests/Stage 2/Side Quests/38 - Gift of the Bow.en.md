# Gift of the Bow

## Summary

Receive a special bow as a gift — first part of the archer chain in Vernworth. The item received unlocks builds and [[39 - A Trial of Archery]].

## How to Unlock

Complete [[37 - Trouble on the Cape]] and return to Vernworth.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[37 - Trouble on the Cape]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Approach **Glyndwr** (elf) near **Roderick's Smithy** in Vernworth.
2. Hand him a **Human-Made Bow** (race-made / elven bows are **rejected**).
3. **Switch your Vocation** to **Archer**, **Magick Archer**, or **Warfarer** before leaving town — he requires you to be wielding a bow.
4. Travel **northwest** to **Glyndwr's training ruins** past Trevo Mine.
5. **Demonstrate bow use** on **banner targets** hanging from the bridge. Glyndwr imitates you.
6. Return to Vernworth and **talk to Glyndwr** near Roderick's Smithy again — unlocks [[39 - A Trial of Archery]].

> [!tip] Fast travel
> Take the **Oxcart from Vernworth to Checkpoint Rest Town** — the ambush attacks place you near the quest location without having to cross Trevo Mine again.
