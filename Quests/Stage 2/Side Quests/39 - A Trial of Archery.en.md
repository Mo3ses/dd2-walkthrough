# A Trial of Archery

## Summary

Archer trial at the Vernworth training ground. Hit targets in sequence or complete a marksmanship challenge using the bow received in [[38 - Gift of the Bow]].

## How to Unlock

Complete [[38 - Gift of the Bow]] and talk to the master archer.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]] (training ground)

## Prerequisites

- [[38 - Gift of the Bow]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to **Glyndwr** near Roderick's Smithy in Vernworth after [[38 - Gift of the Bow]] — quest auto-starts.
2. Travel to **Arborist's Home** in **Sacred Arbor**. **Taliesin** announces the trial is suspended: **Doireann** was taken by an **Ogre** east of the Arbor.
3. Follow the path **east** through **Malachite Forest**, using **white-painted torches** and a **wooden bridge** to descend to the lower area.
4. Meet Glyndwr at the marked entrance, then proceed linearly through the **Ancestral Chamber**, clearing enemies (continue following the white torches).
5. In the large doorway area, **grab the Ring of Percipience** from a chest to the right. Then head left to find **Doireann** and the **Ogre**.
6. **Wait for Glyndwr's arrow to drop Doireann** — then **carry her** (RT) to a safe spot **BEFORE** engaging the Ogre. Defeat the Ogre afterward.
7. **Carry Doireann out** of the chamber; speak with Taliesin to close the quest.
8. **Speak with Taliesin again** to receive the **Hunter's Secret** scroll — unlocks **Heavenly Shot** (Archer skill) for you and your main pawn.

## Objectives

- [ ] Talk to the master archer
- [ ] Receive the trial rules
- [ ] Hit the designated targets
- [ ] Complete the trial
- [ ] Return to receive the reward

## Rewards

- 2,800 XP
- **Repeller Bow**
- **Portcrystal**
- 16,000 G
- **Hunter's Secret** scroll → **Heavenly Shot** Archer skill

(Failed: Doireann dies = no rewards.)
