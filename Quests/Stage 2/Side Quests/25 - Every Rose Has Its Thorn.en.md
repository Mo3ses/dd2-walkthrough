# Every Rose Has Its Thorn

## Summary

Gathering quest for roses / ingredients for a potion or poison. Collect, process, and deliver to Sven or another NPC in Vernworth.

## How to Unlock

Start [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and talk to Sven or a related NPC.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Sven or NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Wilhelmina (Vernworth) — she wants evidence against Allard.
2. **WARNING: time-limited quest** — you have 3 in-game days.
3. Gather the evidence (collect / investigate specific locations).
4. Deliver the evidence to Brant to secure support.
5. Deliver the evidence to Wilhelmina.
6. Talk to Wilhelmina at night (per IGN).
