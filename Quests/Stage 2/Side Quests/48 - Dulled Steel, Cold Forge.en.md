# Dulled Steel, Cold Forge

## Summary

Roman, a blacksmith in the castle gardens of Vernworth, needs help to reignite the castle forge.

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to **Roman** in the castle gardens of Vernworth.
2. If the quest isn't available, try after [[23 - The Stolen Throne]].
