# Spellbound

## Summary

Trysha (Eini's daughter) wants to learn magic. The Arisen needs to help her obtain a spellbook and teach her. Character-focused side quest with cumulative rewards if completed early.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[35 - Beren's Final Lesson]] and visit [[Locations/Eini's House]] (south of Vernworth).

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Trysha
- **Location**: [[Locations/Eini's House]]

## Prerequisites

- [[35 - Beren's Final Lesson]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Go to **Eini's Home** (north of Melve). Talk to Trysha and Eini — if the grandparents block dialogue, pick up Trysha (R2) and carry her outside.
2. **Collect at least 3 of the 5 grimoires needed**:
   - *Let There Be Light* — Myrddin's Home, upstairs desk (enter via outside balcony)
   - *Fulminous Shield* — buy from **Dudley** in Melve
   - *Nation's Death Knell* — chest near the cenotaph in Ancient Battleground — only **2 originals** exist; finish *Tolled to Rest* before distributing
   - *Howling Blizzard* — **Wendy** in the Nameless Village (trade for a forged *Fulminous Shield*)
   - *Towering Earth* — **Waldhar** at The Gracious Hand's Vaults
3. **Tip:** Use **Ibrahim's** forgery service in Checkpoint Rest Town to **duplicate** tomes — originals go to Trysha, copies go to Myrddin in [[43 - The Sorcerer's Appraisal]].
4. **Deliver tomes to Trysha**: give **3 originals at once** to receive the **Turquoise Ring**. Wait and deliver the remaining (optional).
   - **Check:** both grandparents **Eini** and **Eino** must be alive. If the quest stalls, check the morgue in Vernworth and revive with a Wakestone.
5. Wait a few days at the **nearby campsite** (clear enemies first).
6. **Handle the rampage event** — Trysha loses control of magick. **Do NOT attack her**; dodge the spells and press RT to **tackle** when she pauses to recover. Alternatives: cast **Solemnity / High Solemnity**, or **Sedative Bolt** (Magick Archer).
7. Return after a few days — camp again until morning; speak with Trysha and Eini to close.

> [!warning] Pitfalls
> Pawns may **auto-kill Trysha** during the rampage — blocks you from the best rewards. Grandparents (Eini/Eino) can die randomly to local monsters — revive in Vernworth with a Wakestone.

## Objectives

- [ ] Talk to Trysha at Eini's House
- [ ] Obtain the needed spellbook
- [ ] Teach Trysha
- [ ] Return periodically to check progress

## Rewards

- **3 originals + helped control**: 2,000 XP + 3,000 G + **Cast Stone** + **Conjurer's Jottings** (Trysha) + **Enchanter's Almanac** (Eini) — the last two are **affinity-based**, not automatic
- **3 originals at once**: **Turquoise Ring** (extra)
- **Forged tomes**: 2,000 XP + 3,000 G (no ring/affinity items)
