# Saint of the Slums

## Summary

Charitable quest: help the inhabitants of Vernworth's Slums with donations, volunteer work, or mediating conflicts.

## How to Unlock

Start [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and talk to NPCs in the Slums.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Unknown NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]] (Slums)

## Prerequisites

- [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Lady Elena at the Gracious Hand (Slums of Vernworth).
2. After a conversation between Vlasiy and Elena, she asks for a Miasmite (medicinal).
3. Find a Miasmite (item type; Fextralife describes it as a medicinal ingredient) and deliver it.
4. Follow Lady Elena.
5. Investigate the Gracious Hand.
6. Report to Lubomir.
7. Gather evidence and apprehend the abbess (verify outcomes).
