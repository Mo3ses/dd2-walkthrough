# A Beggar's Tale

## Summary

A beggar in the Slums of Vernworth tells a story with a kernel of truth. Investigation reveals a conspiracy tied to the capital's underworld.

## How to Unlock

Start [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and explore the Slums.

- **NPC**: Beggar NPC
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]] (Slums)

## Walkthrough

1. Find a beggar in the Slums of Vernworth (Fextralife).
2. Listen to his story and follow him during the day.
3. Investigate how he earns his money.
4. Discover: he is a noble in disguise.
5. Wait — the quest reveals that you acquired his clothes.
