# Vocation Frustration

## Summary

Introductory questline about the Vocation system in DD2. The Arisen demonstrates confused combat abilities, and a mentor guides them to test and commit to a Vocation.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] and talk to a vocation-related NPC in [[Locations/Vernworth]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Klaus (verify — likely Kendrick or another mentor)
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[10 - A Place to Call Home]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to **Klaus** at the **Vocation Guild** in Vernworth (near the dragon statue, on the way to The Stardrop Inn).
2. Visit **Roderick**, the local armorer — located to the left of the dragon statue, near Shakir's Inn.
3. Travel to **Trevo Mine** via the West Vernworth Oxcart Station or the western gate of Vernworth.
4. Defeat goblins and navigate the mine's narrow paths (Roderick gives routing tips).
5. Pick up the **Archistaff** and **Greatsword** from chests inside the mine.
6. Return both weapons to Klaus at the Vocation Guild — the weapons you deliver determine which **Vocation** unlocks (Warrior or Sorcerer / Magick Archer variants).

## Objectives

- [ ] Talk to the Vocation mentor
- [ ] Test abilities in combat
- [ ] Choose a Vocation path
- [ ] Confirm your choice

## Rewards

- 1,200 XP
- 7,500 G
- Unlocks 2 Vocations (which ones depends on the weapon delivered)
