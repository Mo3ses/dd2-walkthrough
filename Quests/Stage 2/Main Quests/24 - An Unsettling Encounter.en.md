# An Unsettling Encounter

## Summary

A supernatural encounter with an NPC in Vernworth — something is off. How you choose to react affects rewards and reputation.

## How to Unlock

Start [[20 - Disa's Plot]] and speak with Brant at The Stardrop Inn about the "rumors about Allard".

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[20 - Disa's Plot]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Speak with Brant at The Stardrop Inn — ask about the "rumors about Allard".
2. Explanation: Sven was an ally and witnessed a suspicious delivery to Minister Allard, who tried to hide it.
3. Exit the tavern and head to the Royal Quarter / palace.
4. One of Brant's guards will escort you to the Guardhouse.
5. Equip the Marcher's Armor Set and enter.
6. Follow Allard's trail (already known from Disa's Plot).
7. Investigate the suspicious package delivery.
8. Report to Brant.

## Objectives

- [ ] Find the strange NPC
- [ ] Decide how to react
- [ ] Report or confront

## Rewards

- 1,200 XP
- Ring of Accrual
- 5,500 G
- Battahli-Crested Leather (key item, Fextralife)
