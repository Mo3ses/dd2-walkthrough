# Seat of the Sovran

## Summary

Central transition quest from Stage 1 → Stage 2. Auto-triggered when the Arisen crosses the gates of Vernworth after fleeing Melve. The Arisen is formally presented to Sovran Disa and to the political intrigues of the capital.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]] and reach [[Locations/Vernworth]]. (Immediately before, the camouflaged cyclops from [[Side Quests/09 - One-Eyed Interloper]] attacks on the road.)

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant / Sovran Disa
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]] (gates / castle)

## Prerequisites

- [[Main Quests/08 - In Dragon's Wake]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Cross the gates of Melve and take the road to Vernworth (Gregor may die along the way — known bug: jump off the bridge on the left side to reach the Slums and continue to Brant).
2. On arrival at Vernworth, you are mistaken and captured in the Merchant Quarter for identity verification.
3. Brant rescues you and proposes a formal plan — speak with him.
4. Exit the Merchant Quarter: find Norman chasing a man — choose "Lie" or "Tell the Truth" (only affects dialogue; no quest branch).
5. Wait until night (talk to Glenna at The Stardrop Inn, or visit Klaus at the Vocation Guild — visiting Klaus triggers [[19 - Vocation Frustration]] as an optional side).
6. Find Brant at The Stardrop Inn at night and follow him to the private area.
7. Ask Brant about the three threads: Monster Culling, Queen Regent ([[20 - Disa's Plot]]), and Magistrate ([[21 - The Caged Magistrate]]).
8. Accept one of the three missions — this choice **does not** close the other two; you can accept the others later. The point of the quest is just the formal presentation.

## Objectives

- [ ] Cross the gates of Vernworth
- [ ] Be escorted by Brant to the castle
- [ ] Meet Sovran Disa formally
- [ ] Receive royal recognition

## Rewards

No XP/gold. **Gaol Key** (key item) — only if the Magistrate route is accepted.

## Important Notes

> [!warning] Missable: cyclops on the road
> The camouflaged cyclops from [[Side Quests/09 - One-Eyed Interloper]] attacks during the final leg. If you kill Gregor before the cyclops (or vice versa), there are consequences in Vernworth quests.

> [!info] Chain
> Starts the Vernworth political chain. Connects with [[20 - Disa's Plot]] and [[23 - The Stolen Throne]].
