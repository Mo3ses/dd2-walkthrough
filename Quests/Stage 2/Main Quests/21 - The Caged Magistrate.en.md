# The Caged Magistrate

## Summary

A legal quest: a magistrate is imprisoned in Vernworth. Ser Berthold asks the Arisen to investigate the case — judgment and a moral decision are involved.

## How to Unlock

Start [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and speak with Ser Berthold.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Ser Berthold
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] *(started)*

## Walkthrough

1. Receive instructions from Brant at The Stardrop Inn.
2. Go to the Guardhouse (entrance of the palace) and equip the Marcher's Armor Set (obtained in Disa's Plot).
3. Enter the palace through the Guardhouse — turn immediately to the right.
4. Cross to the other side of the palace to the dungeon (jail).
5. Use the Gaol Key to open the double doors and exit through the Slums.
6. Go to the Slums and rescue the former magistrate.
7. Report to Brant.

## Objectives

- [ ] Collect testimonies
- [ ] Decide the magistrate's fate
