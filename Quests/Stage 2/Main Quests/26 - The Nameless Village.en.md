# The Nameless Village

## Summary

Brant directs the Arisen to investigate an unnamed village connected to the political intrigue of Vernworth. Exploration, combat, and a key narrative decision.

## How to Unlock

Complete [[24 - An Unsettling Encounter]] and follow Brant's instructions.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[24 - An Unsettling Encounter]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Meet Brant at The Stardrop Inn after [[24 - An Unsettling Encounter]].
2. Receive the location of the Nameless Village and travel there.
3. Explore the village outskirts — clear any hostiles blocking the path.
4. Investigate the central area; collect clues (documents, NPC testimonies).
5. Confront the leader of the village and make the narrative decision.
6. Report back to Brant in Vernworth.
