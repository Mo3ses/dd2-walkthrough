# Monster Culling

## Summary

Main quest that serves as an extended post-Story-1 combat tutorial. The Arisen is tasked with hunting monsters near Vernworth as official service to the Guard.

## How to Unlock

Start [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] and accept Brant's mission.

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant / Vernworth Guard
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] *(started)*

## Walkthrough

1. Accept the mission from Brant at The Stardrop Inn **at night**, during [[15 - Seat of the Sovran]] — dialogue "Tell me of monster culling" → "Accept".
2. **Trevo Mine** (northwest of Vernworth): go down to the soldiers fighting Goblins and help clear the mine. Speak with Alex on the way out.
3. **Harve Village** (west of Vernworth, south of Trevo Mine): find Dylan near the Rivage Elder's Home and enter **Stormwind Cave**. Kill the Saurians, revive any dead soldiers with a Wakestone (unlocks the "The Savior" achievement), rescue Hollis, and escort the survivors to the exit.
4. **Eastern Edge of Vermund**: travel **during the day** until you find a shipment under attack by Goblins and Saurians; defend it. Speak with the soldiers afterward.
5. Report to Brant at The Stardrop Inn at night (near the bar) to close the quest.

## Objectives

- [ ] Receive the mission from Brant / Guard
- [ ] Cull the designated monsters
- --- after Trevo Mine + Nameless + Harve (and the griffin) ---
- [ ] Return to Vernworth
- [ ] Report to the Guard

## Rewards

- 7,000 G
- 3,000 XP
- Expeditioner's Cloak ×1
