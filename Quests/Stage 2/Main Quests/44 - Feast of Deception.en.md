# Feast of Deception

## Summary

Main quest finale of Stage 2. A banquet in Vernworth hides a larger conspiracy. The Arisen attends, investigates, and confronts the deception. Connects directly to the opening of [[Stage 3]].

## How to Unlock

Complete [[43 - The Sorcerer's Appraisal]] and return to [[Locations/Vernworth]].

## Quest Giver

- **NPC**: Brant / Sovran
- **Location**: [[Locations/Vernworth]]

## Prerequisites

- [[43 - The Sorcerer's Appraisal]] *(completed)*

## Walkthrough

1. Speak with Brant at The Stardrop Inn at night, after [[24 - An Unsettling Encounter]] — he schedules the formal banquet.
2. Wear formal attire (acquired from [[23 - The Stolen Throne]] or [[11 - The Ornate Box]]) and check your dwelling: Brant leaves a gift pack. Return to Brant and say "I am ready".
3. Wait for the night to pass (fast-travel or camp). When Brant heads to the palace, **your Main Pawn warns you** about the **Godsway** — an artifact that lets the false Sovran override your command over Pawns. Sven (blacksmith) confirms the false king wears a strange necklace.
4. Wait **one more day** and return at night. Brant gives you the **Border Entry Permit** (regular or Beastren variant) and reveals that Battahl holds knowledge to counter the Godsway.
5. Attend the banquet, confront the false Sovran's ruse (Brant's original plan was the "Meet the Real Sovran" ceremony, but Vauban/Myrddin's faction used the Godsway — your Pawn is forced to disobey, thwarting the proof of identity).
6. Complete the quest — **Nation of the Lambent Flame** (Stage 3) unlocks automatically. Head to Battahl with the Border Entry Permit.

> [!warning] Chain closure
> Completing this quest **discontinues** [[32 - Readvent of Calamity]] and all other Brant-assigned quests. Do them first.
